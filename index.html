I am a member of the Birmingham Theoretical Computer Science group, and my interests include

  • semantics of programming languages, including denotational, operational and game semantics
  • computational effects, both individually and generally
  • call-by-push-value
  • semantics of nondeterminism
  • category theory, in particular categorical semantics and coalgebra.
If you're interested in PhD research in any of the above topics, please contact me.
